Our mission

StoryCast aims to provide an inclusive platform where high-fidelity audio and video stories are accessible to everyone.

We use research-driven, universal design to ensure that physical or cognitive abilities never limit engagement with powerful narratives.

Accessibility Features

Transcripts

Every audio and video story includes a complete, synchronized transcript for those who prefer to read or need text-based access.

Captions

Video content includes closed captions that are properly timed and clearly formatted for optimal readability.

Keyboard Navigation

All interactive elements are fully accessible via keyboard, with visible focus indicators to track your position.

Screen Reader Support

Our semantic HTML structure and ARIA labels ensure seamless navigation with assistive technologies.

High Contrast

All text and UI elements meet WCAG AA contrast standards for enhanced visibility and readability.

Responsive Design

The platform adapts seamlessly to any device or screen size, ensuring accessibility across all contexts.
