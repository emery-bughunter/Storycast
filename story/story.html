[02:06] BJ Miller: Healthcare was designed with diseases, not people, at its center. Nowhere are the effects of bad design more heartbreaking than at the end of life.

[03:01] BJ Miller: We have a monumental opportunity to rethink and redesign how we die. Let's begin at the end.

[03:22] BJ Miller: For most people, the scariest thing about death isn't being dead; it's suffering. We must distinguish between necessary suffering and unnecessary suffering that we can change.
